📖 How to Play

  1. 🎯 Choose Your Challenge: Enter a word length between 3 and 10 letters
  2. 🚀 Start the Game: Click "Start Game" to begin your adventure
  3. ⌨️ Make Your Guess: Type your guess using your keyboard or click the on-screen keyboard tiles
  4. 👀 Visual Feedback: The on-screen keyboard also shows the current status of each letter
  5. 🎨 Color Clues: Each guess gives you colorful feedback:
    • ✅ Green: Perfect! Correct letter in the correct position
    • 🟡 Yellow: Good! The letter is in the word, but in the wrong position
    • ❌ Red: Nope! The letter is not in the word
  6. 🎲 Five Chances: You have 5 attempts to guess the word
  7. ⏱️ Beat the Clock: Try to guess as quickly as possible and beat your best time!
  8. 💡 Hint Power-ups: Use hints strategically to help you when you're stuck
